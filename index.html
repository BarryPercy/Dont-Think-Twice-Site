God of Carnage

by Yasmina Reza, translated by Christopher Hampton

The Whale Theatre, Greystones

10th & 11th February 2023, 8pm

What happens when two sets of parents meet up to deal with the unruly behaviour of their children? A calm and rational debate between grown-ups about the need to teach kids how to behave properly? Or a hysterical night of name-calling, tantrums and tears before bedtime?

Meet the Actors

John is playing the role of Michel Vallon, husband of Veroniqué and father of Bruno who has been involved in an altercation with Ferdinand Reille. We asked John to describe his character in three words: uncouth, sarcastic, primitive

Mary is playing the role of Veroniqué Vallon, wife of Michel and mother of Bruno who has been involved in an altercation with Ferdinand Reille. We asked Mary to describe her character in three words: Moral crusader, patronising, idealist

Eoghan is playing the role of Alain Reille, husband of Annette and father of Ferdinand who has been involved in an altercation with Bruno Vallon. We asked Eoghan to describe his character in three words: Condescending, Aloof, Rude

Ally is playing the role of Annette Reille, wife of Alain and mother of Ferdinand who has been involved in an altercation with Bruno Vallon. We asked Ally to describe her character in three words: Anxious, suppressed, meek
